About the City

Chandigarh is a union territory and planned city in northern India, serving as the shared capital of the surrounding states, namely Punjab to the north, west and the south, and Haryana to the east. The motif or sobriquet of "The City of Beauty" was derived from the City Beautiful movement, which was a popular philosophy in North American urban planning during the 1890s and 1900s. In this guide, you will find the Tourist Attractions and the ways to travel to the city the easiest and most affordable way possible.

    The top three famous tourist attractions of the city are:
  1. Sukhna Lake
  2. Rock Garden
  3. Japanese Garden
